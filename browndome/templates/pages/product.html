Details

We develop customized legal case management software (LCMs), a legal practice management software that helps law firms/ advocates to digitize their practice and assist them to perform legal research, manage cases, clients, schedule, generate invoices & track billings. LCMs is customized as per the requirements of legal professionals and is equipped with modern tools required in any law firm to efficiently manage different practices.

KEY FEATURES

LCMs has been made with the aim of giving best solution to professionals to efficiently manage their day to day activities.

LCMs helps to do the following activities –

  • Email Management
  • Case Management
  • Litigation management
  • General Counsel (GC) interface
  • Task management
  • Document management
  • Calendar management
  • Timesheet
  • Billing and Invoicing
  • Management Reports

LCMs has a unique role-based and easily customizable dashboard view that allows the users working under different roles to have a quick overview of their open and closed matters as well as billed and non-billed matters. The report module provides senior management with the statistics of their billable and non-billable activities as well. Professionals at senior management level will have different stats as their dashboard view which gives quick overview of the different practices in the firm along with detailed comparison with their past performances

LCMs is developed using Python’s backend framework called Django (version >=2.11) and for the frontend React is used.
