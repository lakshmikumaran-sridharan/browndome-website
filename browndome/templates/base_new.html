About

Browndome consulting is a leading financial advisory firm specialising in investment banking, start up advisory & fund raise, Mergers & acquisitions with a deep set of industry and capital markets relationships.

WHO WE ARE

We are India’s leading private investment banking firm specialising in the consumer, business services, technology and financial services industries.

WHAT WE DO

Start up advisory & fund raise support, Investing in startups, M&A advisory & deal support, Transaction tax, Pre-IPO restructuring, Family business succession planning, developing SaaS based legal tech products

Product

Matter Management Software

We develop customized legal case management software (LCMs), a legal practice management software that helps law firms/advocates to digitize their practice and assist them to perform legal research, manage cases, clients, schedule, generate invoices & track billings. LCMs is customized as per the requirements of legal professionals and is equipped with modern tools required in any law firm to efficiently manage different practices
