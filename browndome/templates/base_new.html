About

Browndome consulting is a leading financial advisory firm specialising in investment banking, start up advisory & fund raise, Mergers & acquisitions with a deep set of industry and capital markets relationships.

WHO WE ARE

We are India’s leading private investment banking firm specialising in the consumer, business services, technology and financial services industries.

WHAT WE DO

Start up advisory & fund raise support, Investing in startups, M&A advisory & deal support, Transaction tax, Pre-IPO restructuring, Family business succession planning, developing SaaS based legal tech products

Services

Our team possesses diversified and significant track record of working with entrepreneurs/ companies offering wide array of services including:

Start up advisory & Fund raise

Business optimisation support and fund raise support

M&A Advisory

Deal sourcing, Strategic deals: Buy side / Sell side, Capital structure

Transaction Tax

Transaction structuring including acquisition, divestment, merger/demerger, inbound & outbound transactions, cross border transactions

Investing in Start-ups

Dedicated focus to back the next wave of ambitious founders and startups at an early stage

KEY FEATURES

  • Transaction structuring including acquisition, divestment, merger/ demerger, capital reduction, slump sale, asset sale, share-buyback, or any other form of internal group restructuring
  • Inbound and outbound investment structuring and funding options
  • Ideation of tax efficient strategies for repatriation of cash surplus/ profits

Product

Matter Management Software

We develop customized legal case management software (LCMs), a legal practice management software that helps law firms/advocates to digitize their practice and assist them to perform legal research, manage cases, clients, schedule, generate invoices & track billings. LCMs is customized as per the requirements of legal professionals and is equipped with modern tools required in any law firm to efficiently manage different practices

KEY FEATURES

  • Email Management
  • Case Management
  • Litigation management
  • General Counsel (GC) interface
  • Task management
  • Document management
  • Calendar management
  • Timesheet
  • Billing and Invoicing
  • Management Reports
