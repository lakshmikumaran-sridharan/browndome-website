SERVICES

Our team has the right functional and industry experience which enables us to understand your business, strategic objectives and provide tailor-made solutions that helps to achieve your business objectives.

Start-up
advisory & fund raise

We assist startups in getting ready for a fund raise and thereafter work as bankers for the entire fund raise cycle

KEY FEATURES

  • Review of the capital structure
  • Review of capex plans and operation strategy
  • Business optimization support - Developing a formal business plan & branding strategy
  • Documentation and closure of fund raise – Investor meetings, terms sheet & SHA negotiations, etc.

M&A
Advisory

Buy & Sell Side - Deal Advisory for acquisition/ divestments of businesses and strategic assets; M&A buy outs

KEY FEATURES

  • Deal sourcing - Source and evaluate strategic fitment of an acquisition target or joint venture
  • Buy & Sell side - Advisory on strategic deals
  • Advisory on capital structure

Transaction
Tax

Ideation of tax efficient & regulatory compliant strategies that help enterprises in managing their business operations and strategic transactions

KEY FEATURES

  • Transaction structuring including acquisition, divestment, merger/ demerger, capital reduction, slump sale, asset sale, share-buyback, or any other form of internal group restructuring
  • Inbound and outbound investment structuring and funding options
  • Ideation of tax efficient strategies for repatriation of cash surplus/ profits
  • Restructuring to raise funds
  • Promoter shareholding rationalization/ restructuring
  • Pre-IPO restructuring - Segregation of non-core businesses/ assets
  • Family business succession planning
